WOMEN IN TECH
GLOBAL CONFERENCE 2022

The women in tech event is conference that brings together women passionate about technology from all over the world. These women will get a chance to showcase their work to fellow women tech enthusiasts. They get a chance to speak out of the challenges they face as women in the tech space. It is an open event where the ladies could speak up of their falures and achievements ever since they joined the industries. We would love to hear every ladie's story from how they started of, how they found out their passion was in technology, the challenges they've faced and all their achievements. We strongly encourage diversity and we are inviting women from all parts of the world. We know that the tech space is wide so we would want everyone to attend, from software engineers, product managers, project managers to quality assuarance people.

Please contact us via email for further questions regarding the women in tech global conference 2022
@womenintechconference@hotmail.com
