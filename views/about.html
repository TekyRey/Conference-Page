GLOBAL CONFERENCE 2022

The women in tech event is conference that brings together women passionate about technology from all over the world. These women will get a chance to showcase their work to fellow women tech enthusiasts. They get a chance to speak out of the challenges they face as women in the tech space. It is an open event where the ladies could speak up of their falures and achievements ever since they joined the industries.
